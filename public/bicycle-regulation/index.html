Bicycle Regulation in Queensland

Do you know the rules of the road? Check out this resource for tips and links to the information you need to know.

Please note that this is not legal advice, and while I’ve listed sources for the information, it may not be correct or applicable in your case.

If you have questions that aren’t answered here, shoot an email to briscycle@kyd.com.au and I’ll try to hunt down the answer for you.

  • Bicycles & Alcohol in Queensland

    Bicycles are considered vehicles under Queensland law, and as such are subject to alcohol laws. The law applies to bikes almost exactly the same as it does to cars.

About Briscycle

Briscycle is a passion project by Ash Kyd. It originated back in 2009 when there was little or no information about (let alone cycling infrastructure) in Brisbane.

Today there are a bunch of resources, including Brisbane Council's cycling website, so this is aiming to be a bit more of a general resource.

The source code to this site is available on github which means with a little technical skill you can help update the site as well! But if you just have a question or comment, please feel free to get in touch by email.
